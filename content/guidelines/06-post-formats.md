# Post Formats

**Format Menu for LinkedIn Content**

Choose the format that best matches your content and goals.

---

## Format Menu

| Format | Best For | Effort |
|--------|----------|--------|
| Feature Demo | Demos, workflows, quick tips | Medium |
| Tutorial | Step-by-step teaching, how-to guides | High |
| Carousel | Frameworks, step-by-step guides, lists | Medium |
| Data Visualization | Data, comparisons, process maps | High |
| Meme | Industry humor, relatable takes, viral potential | Low |
| Chat to Camera | Personal takes, founder POV, direct connection | Medium |
| Industry News Commentary | Hot takes on news, trend analysis, timely reactions | Low |
